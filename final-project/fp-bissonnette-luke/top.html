! ! ! Magic Box ! ! !

Instructions of the game: (vvv scrol down vvv)
The goal of the game is to make the sum of all the numbers in row, column, and diagonal equal to 15. Click on a number in the box to display it, but don't worry because you will always be able to undo it. There is only one solution, and you can reveal it at anytime by clicking on the "Solution" button below. If you would like to start a new game, click on the "New Game" button. Have fun!
