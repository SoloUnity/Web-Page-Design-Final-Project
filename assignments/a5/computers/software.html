The Software in Your Computer


The software in the computer that you're reading this off of right now is a culmination of decades of research and development. Each piece of the puzzle has its own specialised job and is essential day to day tasks performed on the computer. Computer software refers to programs and other operating information that computers use to function. Software is a compilation of clear instructions that tells the hardware to perform specific tasks that it was designed for. Some of the commonly found software found on computers include:
  • Office programs
  • Games
  • Browsers
